Our Story

Crafting Moments, One Roast at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Indiranagar, Aurora Brew House is more than just a coffee shop. We are a sanctuary for coffee purists and casual drinkers alike, sourcing the finest single-origin beans from the lush estates of Chikmagalur.

Every batch is roasted with precision to unlock its distinct flavor profile. We believe that coffee brewing is an art form, and our passionate baristas pour their heart into every cup to deliver a truly memorable experience.

"A sanctuary where craft meets comfort, and every bean has a tale to share."
